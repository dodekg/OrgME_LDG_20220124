Why Care About Organic Food?

The way we farm and eat can make a world of difference. Organic is an 'agroecological' farming system that offers many benefits. Is better for the planet - No artificial fertilisers, Healthier soils, that store more carbon, Meat that's more environmentally friendly. Has higher welfare for farm animals. Is better for nature and wildlife. And is better for People. Food that contains fewer pesticides. Fewer additives and preservatives. No GM ingredients. Food produced using fewer antibiotics. Food from more resilient farms. Producing food that's nutritionally different.

Discover Your Local Suppliers

Having the right supplier is crucial for OrganicME business. There are a number of benefits and advantages for choosing to source products locally. Local suppliers will provide OrganicME with high-quality products. Sourcing locally means that you can have direct oversight of the supplier process. Knowing that everything OrganicME does is local is a huge benefit the customers and the community and therefore OrganicME brand. Products are environmentally Friendly and Sustainable – Sourcing locally is reducing carbon footprint and fuel emissions as it doesn’t take as much fuel to transport goods across the small (local) distances.

Discover Kelso and Scottish Borders

Kelso is a market town in the Scottish Borders area of Scotland. Within the boundaries of the historic county of Roxburghshire, it lies where the rivers Tweed and Teviot have their confluence. The town has a population of 5,639 according to the 2011 census . Kelso's main tourist draws are the ruined Kelso Abbey and Floors Castle a William Adam designed house completed in 1726. The Kelso Bridge was designed by John Rennie who later built London Bridge.
